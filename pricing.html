How billing works

Buttons call /.netlify/functions/generateQuiz with action: "create_checkout_session" so the browser never sees your STRIPE_SECRET_KEY. Map each plan to a Stripe Price using STRIPE_PRICE_ID_* variables documented in README.md.

You can change displayed dollar amounts anytime — Stripe still charges the prices attached to your Price objects.
