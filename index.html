How it works

Three calm steps from idea to quiz.

Designed to match how teachers plan: clarify the outcome, generate a draft, then refine with your professional judgment.

1

Describe the outcome

Add your topic, grade band, difficulty, and the number of questions you want. Keep it specific—better inputs make better classroom materials.

2

Generate securely

Your request is authenticated and processed server-side. Optional caching can speed up repeats while you iterate on wording.

3

Review and teach

Preview questions, distractors, and explanations. Adjust in class, export later, or extend the product with roster features when you are ready.
